About Us

Our team has guitar luthiers with over 10 years of experience who are completely passionate about the art of creating high-quality instruments with top-notch performance. We are always busy sketching guitar designs, sourcing raw woods and materials, spraying guitars in our paint booth, and fine-tuning, testing, and playing our guitars prior to shipping them out to customers all around the world. We've made it fun, efficient and affordable for folks to design, purchase and play their very own pro-grade custom guitar.

Together we will go over every detail, from size and shape, to bindings and rosette design, to pick­ups and tuners. Here at Cithara we build handcrafted acoustic guitars that are unique to each player’s vision and style.

The Process

After hiring us, you will select the guitar model that best suits your aesthetics and playing style. Don't worry: we can guide your decision once we learn a bit about you and the music you play.

We make all the main types of acoustic guitars avaliable: Dreadnought & variants, Parlor, Jumbo, etc.

When you are satisfied with your guitar model, you will look through our tonewoods and select the set of wood you would like. We work with many high-quality wood types, such as Maple, Mahogany, Rosewood, Basswood, Walnut, and more.

Components such as tuners, frets, the bridge and pickups are made out wood, plastic or metal. It's up to you. You can select many different types and styles for each component, all avaliable in our customer catalog.

After we have a project set up exactly the way you want it, we will get your custom acoustic guitar onto our build schedule. You can follow the development of the project with us at any time.
